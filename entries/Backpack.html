Backpacking at Kickerillo-Mischer preserve

Explore the great outdoors at Kickerill-Mischer Preserve, and learn which backpacking essentials you need for an outdoor adventure under the direction of a KMP staff member.

Get unplugged and learn to locate yourself outdoors! Gain experience reading a map and learn how to use a compass, which is a handy skill when exploring the great outdoors without cell service.

Registration and face coverings are required.
